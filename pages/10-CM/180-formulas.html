= Formulas

Mathematical formulas can be entered using the TeX syntax and are typeset by {:KaTeX|https://khan.github.io/KaTeX/function-support.html}. Surround the formulas by ~\{\$\$~ and ~\$\$\}~:

~~~
{$$ 20 \times 10^{0.0002} $$}
~~~
