= Paragraphs

Paragraph detection is automatic. Paragraphs are blocks of text separated by blank lines, headers, code blocks, sections, or horizontal lines. In addition, lines that begin with ~.~ in the first position also mark a beginning of a new paragraph.

~~~
Paragraph,
still the same paragraph.

A new paragraph.
On two source lines.
.A paragraph without a blank line before it.
.Another
\. This is not.
-----
One after a horizontal line.
~~~
