= Tables

The support for tables is very basic: in a fenced section ~table~, ~[~ and ~]~, resp. ~(~ and ~)~ delimit table header cells resp. data cells:

~~~
--- div.left
With borders:
--- table.border
[th1][th2][th3][th4]
(td1)(td2)(td3)(td4)
(td1)(td2)(td3)(td4)
(td1)(td2)(td3)(td4)
---
---

--- div.right
Borderless:
--- table
[th1][th2][th3][th4]
(td1)(td2)(td3)(td4)
(td1)(td2)(td3)(td4)
(td1)(td2)(td3)(td4)
---
---
~~~
