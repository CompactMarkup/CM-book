= Special links
Special links are shortcuts for links that lead to frequently used websites.

$CM supports these special links:

== Wikipedia
Love it or hate it, Wikipedia is here and is often used. To make a simplified, special link to it, use ~wp:~. Spaces in link text are automatically replaced by ~_~, and the text becomes a part of the complete URL:

~~~
An {wp: audio engineer} is a person who
{wp: records sound|Sound recording and reproduction}.
He makes an {wp:analogue|analog recording} or {wp:digital recording}.
~~~
makes {>}
---.box
An {wp: audio engineer} is a person who
{wp: records sound|Sound recording and reproduction}.
He makes an {wp:analogue|analog recording} or {wp:digital recording}.
---
